StationReminder

Your iPhone dings when you approach your Metro station or bus stop — even in your pocket.

    Tap a line to see it on the map, then tap stations to select them — pick as many as you like.

      Pick a route to draw it with its stops — or zoom the map in to see all stops around you. Tap stops to select.

        Map tiles need internet — search and your saved reminders still work offline.

        How this works
        1. Tap stations or bus stops on the map and hit Set up.
        2. For each one, follow the steps to create a “When I arrive” automation in the Shortcuts app (~2 minutes, one time per place).
        3. Done. iOS watches the geofences in the background and dings + announces the place when you get close — no app needs to be open.

        First time? before trusting it on a commute.

        Optional: download the StationReminder Ding shortcut (open the file, tap “Add Shortcut”). It gives every reminder the same ding + spoken announcement, editable in one place. The steps also work without it.

        Heads-up: iOS shares a system pool of ~20 geofences across all location automations — set up the places you actually use, not the whole network.
