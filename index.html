StationReminder

Your iPhone dings when you approach your Metro station — even in your pocket.

How this works
  1. Pick a station below.
  2. Follow the steps to create a “When I arrive” automation in the Shortcuts app (~2 minutes, one time per station).
  3. Done. iOS watches the geofence in the background and dings + announces the station when you get close — no app needs to be open.

First time? before trusting it at a station.

Optional: download the StationReminder Ding shortcut (open the file, tap “Add Shortcut”). It gives every station the same ding + spoken announcement, editable in one place. The steps below also work without it.
